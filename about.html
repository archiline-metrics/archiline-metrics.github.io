About

Built on reference-based estimating.

Archline Metrics grew out of local project work where every estimate was checked against what had actually been built before — not just modeled on paper.

Our approach

Numbers grounded in real projects.

That reference-based habit still shapes how we work today: every takeoff is cross-checked against comparable, real-world builds, not just theoretical quantities pulled from a drawing in isolation.

As the work expanded beyond local projects into architectural design, construction documentation, and full preconstruction services, that same discipline carried over — across residential homes, hospitals, and commercial buildings alike.
